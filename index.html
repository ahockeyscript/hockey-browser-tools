Hockey Browser Tools

NHL Playoffs Bracket Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet to generate an NHL playoffs bracket based on current (2017-18) data. Use on the NHL Standings page, and it will pop up an overlay with the playoff matchups based on the current standings. Click on teams to pick the preferred winner of each series.

Bookmarklet: NHL Playoffs Bracket

NHL Standings Indexes Bookmarklet

Adds indexes to the NHL Standings page. Useful for quickly identifying a team's rank on the League/Conference standings.

Bookmarklet: NHL Standings Indexes

How to Use

Drag and drop the link button to your browser bookmarks/favourites bar, or right click and choose to add to your bookmarks/favourites. Then, when you're on the relevant page, click on the bookmark to activate it.
